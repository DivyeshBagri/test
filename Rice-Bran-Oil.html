About Rice Bran Oil

Kamal is one of the leading manufacturing organisations in the field of specialty oils in India with over three decades of experience. Our goal is to fulfil the nutritional and health needs of families. KSEPL is ISO 22000:2005 certified confirming to Global Food Safety System.

Contrary to common misconceptions about oil, our bodies require certain quantities of it as part of low-fat meals to maintain optimal health. Selecting the right oil for one 's diet is paramount, and Rice Bran oil, derived from the brown layer of rice, emerges as a heart-friendly option due to its unique advantages. This oil is physically refined without the use of chemicals, rendering it completely bland and odorless while retaining essential nutrients like antioxidants, Vitamin E, and Oryzanol.

Its chemical-free refining process ensures purity, making it an ideal choice for health-conscious individuals seeking flavorful yet nutritious culinary experiences.

Why choose Energy Max Rice Bran Oil?

The Perfect Balance: Rice Bran oil stands out as a healthier alternative to many popular edible oils, primarily due to its ideal balance of Saturated Fatty Acids (SFA), Monounsaturated Fatty Acids (MUFA), and Polyunsaturated Fatty Acids (PUFA). Recommended by esteemed health organizations like the WHO and the AHA, it boasts a balanced ratio of 1:1.5:1, making it a preferred choice for numerous households seeking dietary wellness.

Dietary Benefits: Enriched with Vitamin E and Squalene, Rice Bran oil is lactose-free, cholesterol-free, and refined under optimal conditions to ensure zero trans-fat content. Its consumption contributes to increased energy levels, fortified immunity, and improved skin tone, thanks to squalene 's antioxidant properties. Additionally, natural Vitamin E combats free radicals, promoting overall health and vitality.

Oryzanol: A Potent Antioxidant:Oryzanol, a key component found exclusively in Rice Bran Oil, possesses remarkable cholesterol-lowering properties. Scientific studies in both humans and animals confirm its efficacy in reducing LDL (bad cholesterol) levels while elevating HDL (good cholesterol). Furthermore, Oryzanol aids in decreasing plasma triglyceride levels, enhancing cardiovascular health significantly.

Economical &Versatile Usage: Rice Bran oil offers an economical alternative to other healthy oil varieties, such as olive oil. Its versatility extends from everyday cooking to salad dressings and deep frying. Chefs and homemakers alike appreciate its efficiency, as its consumption is generally 15%-20% less compared to other oils, owing to its lower absorption rate during cooking.

Shelf Life &Stability: Boasting a longer shelf life and enhanced stability at higher cooking temperatures, Rice Bran oil shines in culinary applications requiring high heat. With a high smoke point of 254 degrees Celsius, it is ideal for grilling, deep frying, and other high-temperature cooking methods, ensuring that food remains crisp, flavorful, and nutritious.

Lyso-lecithin is the by-product of enzymatic degumming process during refining. It can  can be used in bakery, foods, poultry, leather and other industries.

De-oiled rice bran

High in protein, is used in animal feed manufacturing with consistent quality and nutritional value maintained through innovative processes.

Rice bran Fatty Acids

They are used as emulsifiers in manufacturing cosmetics, soaps, and other chemicals with specified properties like FFA, color, oleic content, and acid value.

Rice bran wax

It a dewaxing by-product, has diverse applications like coatings, cosmetics, and food emulsions with specific properties.
