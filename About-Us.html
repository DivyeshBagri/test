Kamal Solvent Extractions Pvt Ltd : A Brief History

Kamal Solvent Extraction Pvt. Ltd. is a rice bran oil manufacturing unit established by entrepreneurial vision of Mr. Damodar Das Mundra and his sons Anil and Sunil Mundra in the year 1988, with more than 50 years of experience in the field of rice bran, he comes from humble roots but immense passion. Started with a capacity of just 50 MTD, Kamal now has a capacity of more than 1000 tons of food processing per day.
